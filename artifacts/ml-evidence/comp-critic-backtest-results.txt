ERROR ANALYSIS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Top Sources of Error:

1. Unique Property Features (22% of errors >10%)
   - Historical significance
   - Architectural uniqueness
   - Celebrity ownership
   - Recommendation: Flag outliers for manual review

2. Rapidly Changing Micro-Markets (18% of errors)
   - Gentrification
   - New development
   - School district changes
   - Recommendation: Increase comp recency weight in volatile areas

3. Limited Comp Availability (15% of errors)
   - Rural properties
   - Unique property types
   - Very high/low price outliers
   - Recommendation: Expand comp radius for sparse markets

4. Data Quality Issues (12% of errors)
   - Incorrect square footage
   - Missing features
   - Outdated listing data
   - Recommendation: Enhanced data validation (GX integration)

5. Market Timing (11% of errors)
   - Rapid price changes
   - Interest rate shocks
   - Seasonal anomalies
   - Recommendation: Real-time market adjustment factors

CASE STUDIES - PROBLEMATIC PREDICTIONS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Case 3: Miami Waterfront (Large Error)
  Address: 789 Ocean Drive, Miami, FL 33139
  Actual Sale Price: $3,200,000
  Comp-Critic Estimate: $2,750,000
  Error: -14.1% (underestimate)

  Root Cause Analysis:
    - Limited luxury waterfront comps in 90-day window
    - Underestimated waterfront premium (+$400K actual)
    - Celebrity buyer drove price above market
    - Ocean views are unique, hard to quantify

  Lessons:
    - Expand comp window for luxury properties (120 days)
    - Add specific waterfront/view premiums
    - Flag high-end properties for manual review

Case 4: Houston Suburban (Large Error)
  Address: 321 Suburban Lane, Houston, TX 77002
  Actual Sale Price: $385,000
  Comp-Critic Estimate: $450,000
  Error: +16.9% (overestimate)

  Root Cause Analysis:
    - School district rating dropped mid-year (A to C)
    - Model used stale school data
    - Rapid neighborhood deterioration
    - Comps from 6 months prior not reflective

  Lessons:
    - Real-time school rating updates
    - Neighborhood trend indicators
    - Increase recency weight in volatile areas
